   chmod using Numeric characters:

Number                    Represents                                      Status

  0                              No permission                                   --
  1                               Execute Permission                       --x
  2                              Write Permission                             -w-
  3                               Execute and write                         -wx
  4                              Read Permission                              r--
  5                              Read,Execute                                   r-x
  6                              Read,Write                                        rw-
  7                              Read,Write and Execute            rwx
